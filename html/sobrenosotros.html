About Us

Welcome to Xene Burguer, where passion for delicious burgers meets a vision of creating a community hub for food enthusiasts. Our journey began in the bustling streets of the United States, where two friends, Ruben and Alejandro, shared a dream of crafting the perfect burger experience.

Driven by a desire to offer more than just a meal, we set out to infuse our burgers with creativity, quality ingredients, and a dash of nostalgia. From the sizzle of the grill to the warmth of our hospitality, every aspect of Xene Burguer reflects our dedication to delivering an exceptional dining experience.

But our story doesn't end with delicious burgers. We believe in giving back to the community that has welcomed us with open arms. That's why we actively participate in local initiatives and strive to create a space where everyone feels like family.

Join us on this flavorful journey as we continue to innovate, create, and spread joy one bite at a time. Thank you for choosing Xene Burguer, where every burger tells a story.

Join Our Team

Are you passionate about creating unforgettable culinary experiences? Do you thrive in a dynamic and fast-paced environment? Join our team at Xene Burguer and become a part of our journey to deliver exceptional food and hospitality to our community.

We are always looking for dedicated individuals who share our love for burgers and commitment to excellence. Whether you're a seasoned chef, a friendly server, or a behind-the-scenes wizard, there's a place for you at Xene Burguer.
